Making Lost-and-Found Easier at KDU

This prototype helps students report lost or found items online. Instead of depending only on friends, notice boards, or social media, students can search all reported items in one place.

Report Lost Items

Students can submit information about an item they lost, including item name, category, location, date, and contact details.

Report Found Items

Students or staff can post found items so the real owner can search and claim them.

Search and Filter

Users can search items by name, category, location, or description to find possible matches quickly.

Claim Verification

The system can ask claim questions to reduce fake claims and protect item owners.
